Dataset Statistics: Largest Alzheimer EEG dataset

Total number of subjects: 241
Number of Healthy Controls (HC): 78
Number of Alzheimer’s Disease (AD) subjects: 122
Number of EEG channels: 19
Sampling rate (Hz): 128
Recording duration: 101,916 seconds total (~28.3 hours). Average duration per subject is ~423 seconds.
Shape of the data matrix: 19 Channels × 128 Time samples per epoch.
